Safe Trading Guide

CloudSentinel MarketLink connects buyers and sellers, but every user must trade carefully. Please follow these safety tips before making any transaction.

Important: Do not send money in advance unless you personally trust the seller. Meet in a public market, shop, office, or secure physical location. Inspect the item before making payment.

🤝 Meet Physically

Meet sellers in open and safe places such as markets, shops, offices, or public business areas.

🔍 Inspect Before Payment

Check the product carefully before paying. Confirm the quality, condition, and ownership.

💸 Avoid Advance Payment

Do not send money before seeing the item, unless the seller is someone you personally trust.

🚩 Report Suspicious Listings

Be careful with prices that look too cheap, fake pictures, or sellers refusing physical meetings.
